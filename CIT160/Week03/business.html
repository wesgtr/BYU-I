Fraction of Cars

The dream of having your Sports Car is closer than you think. With Fractions of cars, you get quota from the car of your dreams.


On average, sports vehicles are used only 40 days a year (already counting the days of the week). In the Fraction of Cars model, you purchase your favorite car with two more shareholders, sharing all expenses and using it according to a previously established schedule. You can also have more shares of the same car or several shares of several cars, changing weekly and taking advantage of all types of sports available.
